◆ Eat Omega-3 Fatty Acids.

Omega-3 fats, healthy fats, can not only relieve symptoms of depression, but also help maintain the level of brain chemistry, such as dopamine and serotonin.
Omega-3 fatty acids can not be produced in the body, so they must be absorbed through the food.


[Recommended Food]

Fish: Herring, Sardine, Mackerel, Salmon, Flatfish
Vegetables: Brussels cabbage, Spinach, Kale
Oil: Linseed oil, Canola oil, Soybean oil, Mustard oil
Egg, Walnut



◆ Check the Level of Vitamin D

The easiest way to get vitamin D is to get direct sunlight into your skin. However, it is possible to eat through food.


[Recommended Food]
